2026/S 042-110823 — Cross-border digital identity framework (EUDIW-aligned)

Official Journal reference. Open procedure. Multi-country consortium statement required.

Section I — Contracting authority
The European Regional Innovation Office (ERIO), Brussels, on behalf of the participating Member States under the EUDIW interoperability framework. Lead buyer: Bundesamt für Sicherheit in der Informationstechnik (BSI), Bonn, DE.

Section II — Object

  • TitleCross-border digital identity framework (EUDIW-aligned)
  • Reference number2026/S 042-110823
  • CPV code72000000 — IT services: consulting, software development, Internet and support
  • Project nameEU Digital Identity Wallet cross-border federation framework
  • CategoryInformation technology — digital identity and trust services
  • Delivery locationBrussels (consortium), with member-state delivery in DE, FR, IT, ES, NL
  • Contract duration48 months
  • Scope descriptionDesign, development, and operation of a cross-border identity federation layer aligned with the EUDIW architecture reference framework, including attribute providers, relying party adapters, and conformity assessment tooling.
  • Eligibility requirementsConsortia are allowed. Each member must demonstrate prior implementation of eIDAS-2 aligned identity solutions. Subcontracting permitted up to 30% of the contract value.
  • Evaluation criteriaTechnical approach (40%), consortium experience (25%), price (25%), sustainability and accessibility (10%).
  • Submission languageEnglish (working language); national-language attachments accepted.

Section III — Financial

  • Budget valueEUR 24,000,000 (framework agreement ceiling, 4 years)
  • Procedure typeOpen procedure, framework agreement with multiple economic operators
  • LotsThree lots: (1) core wallet reference implementation, (2) attribute provider federation, (3) conformity assessment tooling
